Meet The Guilds Meet The Guilds

In a world of six guilds, each with their own unique skills and abilities, only one can be crowned the winner. Choose your guild wisely and lead your army to victory in this epic battle for supremacy.

Archers Guild Archers Guild

The Archers are a fearsome group of warriors who relied on their greatwood bows to shoot volley after volley of deadly arrows into the sky. They are so skilled in their craft that they could shroud the sky in darkness with their attacks.

Assassins Guild Assassins Guild

The Assassins, a secretive order of warriors who use chakrams as their signature weapon, are known for their deadly precision and unparalleled skills. But what is the story behind these enigmatic warriors? Who were they and where did they come from?

Barbarians Guild Barbarians Guild

The Barbarians, with their earth-shattering hammers, are a fierce and fearless warriors. They have a natural strength and power that makes them formidable opponents. They have a fierce love of freedom and are not afraid to fight for what they believe in.

Knights Guild Knights Guild

The history of the Knights is one full of bloodshed and destruction. the Knights have defended their land with their swords. They are known for their chivalry and sense of honor. Their swords are legendary and their skills as warriors unsurpassed.

Priests Guild Priests Guild

The Priests, with their holy fervor, march to the battlefield. They believe that by sacrificing themselves they can help their kingdom achieve victory. This sacrificial behavior has made the priests some of the most feared warriors in all of history.

Wizards Guild Wizards Guild

The Wizards have been feared for their devastating fire. Some say it was given to them by a god, others say they've just had a lot of practice. But no one knows for sure how they produce the flames that can incinerate everything in their path. Whatever the case may be, these powerful magicians are a force to be reckoned with.

About The Game About The Game

Guilds n Glory is an upcoming battle action game that allows players to command an army and compete for glory. With detailed 3D graphics and an action-packed gameplay, Guilds n Glory immerses players in the thick of battle. Players can choose from a variety of guilds, each with their own unique abilities, to conquer the opposition.
